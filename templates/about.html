What We Do?

Spark Hydraulics is specialized in the repair of hydraulic pumps, cylinders, motors and valves. Our professional hydraulic repair capabilities are at the core of the widespread services that we provide, from a simple resealing of a hydraulic cylinder to a major overhaul.

Where Are We?

Established in the year 2008, Spark Heavy Hydraulic Equip, Maint L.L.C is located at Industrial Area 3 in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates.
